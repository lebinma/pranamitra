About Us

Nikhil Jose

Founder


Vision

To achieve a healthy living environment and healthy people.


Mission

To create awareness about the healthcare and become pioneer in initiating health services that is exceptional, high quality and affordable to everyone.


Aims and Objectives of The Trust

1. To create awareness among the people about the various health issues and availability of modern health care facilities.
2. To guide and facilitate both domestic and international patients for proper health care.
3. Fight against diseases especially cancer, cardiac and other life threatening diseases.
4. To help in eradicating communicable diseases from the society.
5. To conduct awareness camps for early diagnosis of diseases, to facilitate treatments and for vaccinations.
6. To organize blood donation camps and to encourage organ donation. 7. To become part in programs that aims in a pollution free and eco- friendly environment so as to provide a healthy living atmosphere for the people.
8. To engage in programs to create awareness about road safety and prevent road accidents.
9. To establish, develop, maintain and run hospitals, dispensaries, maternity homes, child welfare centres, old age homes, orphanages,homers for destitute women and mentally retarded children and such other similar medical and healthcare institutions in the country.
10. Help to promote health of the people, especially for weaker and economically poor sections suffering from cancer, kidney deceases and other similar diseases through arranging/ availing medical facilities and medicines at lower prices or free of costs in deserving cases and by arranging health camps, health awareness programs, yoga camps, setting up medical institutions, clinics, dispensaries, medical shops etc. including mobile unit of the same to make available the services to the bedridden patients at their residences.
11. To promote awareness against the use of alcohol and drugs and to set up counselling centres to rehabilitate the addicted persons.
12. To aid, establish, take over or collaborate with any institution or association, having identical aims and objectives as that of the trust.
13. To establish any institution for the welfare of victims of natural calamities and to provide food and shelter to needy persons from time to time including the maintenance and development of orphans of any community or society.
